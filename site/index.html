What is Sendspin?

Sendspin enables synchronized multi-room audio playback across ESPHome devices. Multiple Satellite1 units can play audio in perfect sync throughout your home.

This fork is a development project to bring Sendspin support to the Satellite1 hardware.

Project Goals

  • Sendspin integration for synchronized multi-room audio
  • Work towards upstream ESPHome component integration
  • Feature parity with Home Assistant Voice PE

Requirements

  • Google Chrome or Microsoft Edge (Web Serial required)
  • USB cable connected to your Satellite1
  • Home Assistant with Voice Assistant configured
  • ESPHome 2026.1.1 or newer

First Time Installation

The device will be erased during first install. After flashing, connect to the "satellite1" WiFi network to configure your home network.
